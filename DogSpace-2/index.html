Hey There!

My name is Mickey Blue, and I'm a five-year-old Australian Shepherd. I was born and raised in New Orleans, LA. Despite the heat, you can catch me outside nearly all the time! I love swimming, running, doing tricks, and competing in obstacle courses. Follow me to read my blog, where I write all about my training.

What I Do

Being active is the most important thing in my life. Playing for leisure is fun, but my real passion is competing. I train at a bare minimum 25 hours per week, depending on my owner or trainer's availability, for all kinds of competitions. I have attended numerous dog shows in the southern region to show off my abilities in tricky obstacle courses, and I have always placed in the top ten — even the top five on several occasions. Where I train

The answer is just about anywhere! If there's a big space, whether indoors or outdoors, then I can train. Typically I can be found training indoors, however, in a similar environment to that of a professional competition. This requires my owner and I to rent out space at our local gym — sometimes I get a discount for being adorable! But I can train outside just as well as I can train inside. My owner and I have a HUGE yard, where we set up all kinds of obstacle courses and practice runs.
