HI!

My name is Mikki Blue. I'm a young, highly energetic Australian Shepherd who loves to play and do tricks. I live with my family in New Orleans, LA. Despite the heat, you can find me outside playing fetch nearly every day. I also enjoy learning new tricks and testing my agility in obstacle courses!

My favorite places to go are:

  1. The Beach
    • Gulfport is the closest good beach to me.
    • The BEST beach I ever went to was Destin, Florida!
  2. The Park
    • Pelican Park's dog park is awesome.
    • ...But any big open space will do!
  3. My own backyard!

If you love playing and the outdoors as much as I do, then I would love to meet you! Follow me on Instagram and Facebook so we can be friends! Also, sub to my YouTube channel for some awesome vids of my agility training and obstacle courses!
